Saturn

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and closest to the Sun. Its surface is similar to Earth's Moon, covered with craters. Mercury has a very thin atmosphere and experiences extreme temperature changes.

  • Diameter: 4,880 km
  • Orbit Period: 88 days
  • Moons: None
  • Average Temperature: 167°C
