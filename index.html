Uranus

Uranus, an ice giant, is known for its unique blue-green color and extreme axial tilt, causing it to rotate on its side. Its distinctive tilt and faint ring system make it a fascinating planet in the outer solar system.

Neptune

Neptune, the farthest planet from the Sun, is distinguished by its deep blue color and strong winds, the fastest in the solar system. Its dynamic weather patterns and faint rings make it an intriguing ice giant.
